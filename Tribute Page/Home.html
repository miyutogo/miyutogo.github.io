One of the most successful female streamers, Imane anys, better known as Pokimane, was born in Morocco on 14 May 1996. She is a Moroccan-Canadian who also attended McMaster University. Pokimane dropped out of her Chemical Engineering degree to pursue streaming full time on Twitch. She has since established herself as a content creator on platforms like Twitch, Youtube, and Tiktok. She is also part of Offline TV - an online social entertainment group of content creators based in Los Angeles, California.

Pokimane has been in the streaming industry since 2013. She was then the receipient of Best Streamer of the Year Award at The Shorty Awards in 2018. Pokimane is part of the top 10 most followed streamer in Twitch. Naming her the most followed female streamer in Twitch.

Pokimane has always been the type of person to give back to the community. She then decided to give back to the twitch community by donating $10,000 to random streamers. Her selection, she explains, was based on two criteria: "Either to streamers and communities that I already know and inspired me or to some newer communities that I'm just discovering now." She always try her best to give back every now and then even outside the streaming community.
