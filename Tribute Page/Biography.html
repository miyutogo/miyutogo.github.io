Establishing herself on Twitter did not take her long, because she already had a YouTube page. She has currently more than 357,000 followers on Twitter. Also, Pokimane is immensely popular with Instagram, where she has over 790,000 followers. She also has an official Facebook account that she uses to communicate with her friends and supporters.
